## 一、4 个声音练习段落(英文)

来自 Feishu 课件——专门用于 [[wiki/03-公众演讲-说清楚/半即兴演讲/Delivery-声音|Voice — Melody]] 训练。

**训练方法**:
1. **Read stories** —— 朗读这些段落,练习 Volume / Pitch / Tempo 起伏
2. **[[Shadowing八步法|Shadowing]]** —— 找到对应口音的录音(政客演说 / TED 风格)跟读

### 段落 1:Lifelong Learning(终身学习)

> *In today's rapidly evolving workplace, the only constant is change. To stay relevant, we must embrace lifelong learning. Whether it's mastering new technologies or developing soft skills, our ability to adapt will define our success. Remember, the most valuable asset you can invest in is yourself.*

**重点训练**:
- 重音:*the only constant is change*(对比强调)
- 停顿:**Whether** 之前一拍 / *adapt* 之后一拍
- 收尾:*the most valuable asset* 拉慢

### 段落 2:Collaboration(团队协作)

> *Great achievements are rarely the result of individual effort. In our organization, we thrive on collaboration. Each team member brings unique strengths, and it's through our collective efforts that we overcome challenges and achieve our goals. Together, we are stronger than the sum of our parts.*

**重点训练**:
- 起势:*Great achievements* 慢起,蓄势
- 节奏:中间 *brings unique strengths* 加速
- 收尾:*stronger than the sum of our parts* 重音 + 拉慢(Refrain 收尾感)

### 段落 3:Leadership(领导力)

> *True leadership is not about commanding; it's about inspiring and empowering others. As leaders, our role is to create an environment where everyone feels valued and motivated to contribute their best. When we lead with empathy and vision, we unlock the full potential of our teams.*

**重点训练**:
- 对比:*not about commanding* / *it's about inspiring* —— 对比清晰
- 节奏:*everyone feels valued and motivated* 三拍 + 重音连续
- 收尾:*unlock the full potential* 重音

### 段落 4:Career Success(职业成功)

> *A successful career is built on clear goals and consistent effort. Start by defining your aspirations, then break them into actionable steps. Remember, it's not about reaching the destination overnight, but making progress every day. With focus and perseverance, you can achieve anything you set your mind to.*

**重点训练**:
- 节奏对比:*clear goals* (短) / *and consistent effort* (略长)
- 翻转:*not about reaching the destination overnight* / *but making progress every day* —— 强对比
- 收尾:*anything you set your mind to* —— 提气收

## 二、5 个即兴问题(场景题)

来自 Feishu 课件——专门用于 [[即兴演讲/结构-5框架|即兴演讲 5 框架]] 训练。

**训练方法**:
1. **30 秒读完** + **判断属于哪类问题**
2. **30 秒挑框架 + 准备**
3. **2 分钟口头讲完**
4. 录音回听 + 反馈(自评对照框架是否完整,或请同事/教练点评)

### 题 1:What does it take to become a good leader?

**推荐框架**:[[PREP-Point-Reason-Example-Point|PREP]]

**思路提示**:
- Point:好领导的核心是 X(自己挑一个观点)
- Reason:为什么 X 重要(职场痛点 / 团队需求)
- Example:举一个具体案例(自己的老板 / 见过的领导)
- Point:重申 X

### 题 2:Do we need to learn English when AI can help us translate?

**推荐框架**:[[PREP-Point-Reason-Example-Point|PREP]] 或 [[Comparison-Contrast-Conclusion|CCC]]

**思路提示**(用 PREP):
- Point:要学,而且更要学
- Reason:AI 能翻字面但翻不出关系和判断
- Example:跟 native 沟通的微妙场景(反讽 / 文化暗示 / 实时调整)
- Point:学英语不是为了翻译,是为了感知关系

### 题 3:Inefficient meetings are draining team energy: What should you do as a manager?

**推荐框架**:[[Problem-Solution-Benefit|Problem-Solution-Benefit]]

**思路提示**:
- Problem:开会多但没产出 / 团队疲惫
- Solution:议程 + 决策项 + 主持人(三项不全不开)
- Benefit:每周省时间 / 决策更快 / 团队战斗力恢复

### 题 4:Internal Promotion vs. External Recruitment: How to balance executive selection?

**推荐框架**:[[Comparison-Contrast-Conclusion|CCC]](详见 [[Comparison-Contrast-Conclusion]] 例 1)

**思路提示**:
- Comparison(内部晋升):熟悉文化 / 上手快 / 视角单一
- Contrast(外部招聘):新视角 / 注入资源 / 适应期长
- Conclusion:看公司当前最缺什么——两条腿走路

### 题 5:Describe a challenging case you encountered.

**推荐框架**:[[STAR-Situation-Task-Action-Result|STAR]](详见 [[STAR-Situation-Task-Action-Result]] 例 1)

**思路提示**:
- Situation:背景(团队 / 项目 / 时间)
- Task:你的具体任务 / 难点
- Action:你做的 1-3 个关键动作
- Result:结果 + 学到什么(数据 + 反思)

## 训练建议

### 声音段落训练计划

**冷启动**(第 1 周):
- 每天选 1 个段落,**朗读 5 遍**(自己录音)
- 第 5 遍后回听,标记**节奏不对的地方**

**进阶**(第 2-4 周):
- 加上 [[Shadowing八步法|Shadowing]]
- 找对应主题的英文演讲(TED Talks / 政客演说),做 4 个段落对应的影子跟读

### 即兴问题训练计划

**冷启动**(第 1 周):
- 每天 1 题,**只用 PREP**(熟悉一个框架)
- 录音回听 + 检查是否有完整的 P-R-E-P

**进阶**(第 2-4 周):
- 5 个框架轮流套(每天换一个)
- **30 秒抽签 + 直接讲**——模拟真实即兴

**高阶**(第 5 周+):
- 朋友 / 同事**当场出题**(不预告)
- **30 秒选框架 + 即讲**——接近真实临场

### 跟 Mini-Presentation 配合

5 个即兴问题做熟后,可升级到 [[Mini-Presentation训练方法论]]:
- 给一个完全陌生的主题
- 30 秒准备 + 3 分钟完整讲完
- **要求覆盖 PoDs + Q&A 应对**——综合训练
